Upcoming Events

1.Belvarosi Festival

Belvarosi or commonly known as Belfeszt is a free music festival which happens in the inner streets of Budapest. Starting off from the Erzsébet tér to Szabadság tér, this 3 day festival creates a musical vibe around the city. The music lovers will immerse themselves into the festivities while enjoying the jazz, rock, pop and folk concerts which are held at the downtown. There are a lot of events which takes place in Budapest during the summer, and the Belvarosi festival is one of the best to kick-off summer. Event Dates: 17th – 23rd May 2021

2.Budapest Spring Festival

In the list of best Budapest music festivals, this festival has made its own mark. Showcasing a musical blend of classical music, jazz, opera and folk music, this festival is considered as the largest annual music festival held in Hungary where participants from Hungary and other international destinations come to participate.Event Dates: 3rd – 19th April 2021

3.Budapest Summer Festival

Summer Festival If you’re visiting Budapest during summertime, then make sure to not miss out this lively event which marks the beginning of summers. From the open theatres, live dance shows, and electrifying concerts makes this event worth a visit. These events takes place at two locations in Budapest, which are Margaret Island and Városmajor and a crowd of around 3000 people come over to witness these exciting shows.Event Dates: 23rd – 26th July 2021

4.Budapest Pride

At this time of the year, the streets of Budapest paints with the vibrance of rainbow colors as various LGBT groups comes out to celebrate the Budapest Pride. During this time, various films documented on the life of lesbians, gay and transgenders are broadcasted. This week-long Budapest Pride film festival has theatre performances, film screenings, and discussions which is followed by a picnic in the city park, live music and stage performances.Event Dates: 3rd July 2021 Suggested Read: 10 Day Trips From Budapest That’ll Add A Hint Of Adventure To Your Trip

6.Oktoberfest Budapest

The Oktoberfest is one of the favorite events in Budapest to celebrate the all-time favorite malt beverage. For the beer lovers this fest is indeed a haven where one can find 100 domestic and international beer types. From the famous and well-known breweries to the less-known local ones, all of them showcase their specialities at the city park.Event Dates: NA

7. Budafok Champagne And Wine Festival

The Budafok Champagne And Wine Festival is one of the finest events in Budapest which happens every year at Budafok which the vast wine cellar in the city. It is one of the most popular wine festivals in Budapest where the visitors can taste of some vintage wines from the well-known as well as lesser-known cellars. This two-day long festival also has a live concerts and many programs to keep the kids entertained.Event Dates: NA
